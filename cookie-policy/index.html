What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser at the request of a website or app. They contain identifiers and settings that allow the service to recognize your session and remember certain actions. On platforms operated under rolldorado, cookies may be set by us or by selected partners providing analytics or embedded content. When you access features branded roll dorado, similar technologies such as local storage or SDKs may be used to achieve the same outcomes. Some cookies expire when you close the browser, while others persist for a defined period to support preferences or security. You can usually manage them via your browser settings or device controls designed for privacy.

We use cookies to run core functions like authentication, load balancing, and fraud prevention that keep sessions stable. Performance and analytics help us understand which pages are popular and whether features perform as expected. On rolldorado, preference cookies remember language, region, and display options so the service feels consistent across visits. Marketing technology on roll dorado may measure campaign reach and avoid showing the same message too many times.
